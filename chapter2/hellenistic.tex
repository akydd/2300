\section{Hellensistic Period}
The wars of the Diadochi, the successors of Alexander the Great,  mark the start of the Hellenistic Period.

The Diadochi were
\begin{itemize}
    \item Craterus
    \item Antipater
    \item Somatophylakes
    \item Macedonian satraps
    \item Royal family
    \item Non-Macedonian satraps and generals
\end{itemize}
